Beef Stroganoff

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Mushrooms
  • Beef Broth
  • Sour Cream
  • Egg Noodles

Directions

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef and onion in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes; drain and discard grease. Stir garlic into beef mixture and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  2. Stir mushrooms into beef mixture and cook until mushrooms are tender, 5 to 7 minutes. Pour beef broth over beef mixture, bring to a simmer, and cook until thickened, about 8 minutes
  3. Stir sour cream into beef mixture and cook until heated through, 2 to 3 minutes. Serve over egg noodles.
